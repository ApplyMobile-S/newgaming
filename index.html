With the next generation of video game console coming this soon this year. The Xbox one and Playstation 4, which will both be on store shelves late in the year. Console gamers will have a dramatic surge forwards in graphics, yet again allowing them to compete with a powerful gaming PC.

We all know PC has the most powerful and will always win in that, but games are exclusive for each platform. With this in mind, us gamers will always have something to look forward to. New content or same game with a new skin, but something we all agree on is that when that game loads we feel restless until we beat at least the first few levels.

Gamers come in every shape and size, and with that every other game designed for that frame in mind. With all the Levels, stages, and puzzles all have some self gratification to keep gamers continuously playing, so why binge on one like Burger King once said, "have it your way". Well for one everyone has a budget so keep that in mind when you make your choice.

Being a PC gamer myself, and bias towards the consoles games it would be an understatement not to say, "PC has more games". PC games offer a much more flexible alternative for gamers, with content more robust and depth in nature. Be choosy since not all games are cross-platform.

You can expect gamers will gravitate towards the games they love, whether its Call of Duty, Battlefield, or World of Warcraft this coming year.
